Who am I

My journey of
becoming me

Well, I figured it was about time to introduce myself!
My name is Brylie, and here are a few things about me!

Once a New Yorker and moved to Michigan, I find myself living in Cookville Tennessee and in love with capturing every moment with my camera! I'm all about living my faith as a follower of Christ, feeling incredibly grateful for everything He's given me. Right now, I work as a baker at Soul Craft Coffee in downtown Cookville – it's where I get to indulge my love for coffee and matcha, especially when they're iced and topped with oat milk! Besides that, I'm a total music and art enthusiast. Thanks for dropping by – your support means the world to me!
